Bio
MR3 is born at the hands of Manny Rodarte III and his passion for music with a mix of pop, rock, funk, R&B and a touch of blues. Manny is an accomplished saxophone and guitar player, a father of 6 beautiful children who has had a trajectory of fifteen years playing with bands he has put together. In learning about his past he said, ”it has been an amazing ride. These past years have been great schooling.” Along the way he has written over 1000 songs! This is only a glimpse of what we know will be an even greater ride. We invite you to be a part of what is going to be an epic journey in the future of MR3.
